Good Standing Certificate

Following documents must be submitted / attached with the application form:
1. Two (2) colored photocopies of Registration Certificate Copy (A or B).
2. Two (2) colored photocopies of VISA or application of foreign exams.
3. Two (2) colored photocopies of passport.
4. Two (2) colored photocopies of CNIC.
5. Hand written application with Registration No. Current Address, purpose for issuance of Good Standing Certificate along with required authority address.
6. Authority letter in case the applicant is living abroad.
7. Pay Order / Demand Draft Registration Fee in the name of Secretary, Punjab Pharmacy Council of Rs. 1000/- for each case .
8. Valid Renewal Status of the Registration certificate.
9. In case of living abroad the above required Scanned Data along with request shall be sent on the email address of Punjab Pharmacy Council i.e.
(punjabpharmacycouncil@hotmail.com)
